# Sustainable Web Design

---

> At a time when we need to be moving rapidly towards a zero-carbon economy, our hunger for data and web services is growing ever greater as are our internet emissions. A 2018 paper published in the Journal of Cleaner Production estimated that communication technology will use **14 percent** of global electricity by 2040, up from just under 4 percent in 2020

[Assessing ICT global emissions footprint: Trends to 2040 & recommendations](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S095965261733233X)

---

![](./images/country-ranking.png)

Data for 2018 shows that when viewed as a whole, **the internet is equivalent to one of the world's most polluting countries**.

---

![](./images/sustainable-design.jpg) <!-- .element: style="height: 33vh" -->

**Sustainable web design** is an approach to designing web services **that prioritizes the health of our home planet**. 

---

![](./images/lower%20CO2.jpg) <!-- .element: style="height: 33vh" -->

At its **core** is a focus on reducing **carbon emissions and energy consumption**.

## Sustainable Web Manifesto

![](./images/Sutainable%20Web%20Manifesto.png) <!-- .element: style="height: 33vh" -->

[sustainablewebmanifesto.com](https://www.sustainablewebmanifesto.com/)

---

The Manifesto consists of 6 Rules.

---

### 01. Clean

> The services we provide and services we use will be powered by renewable energy.

[sustainablewebmanifesto.com](https://www.sustainablewebmanifesto.com/)

---

### 02. Efficient

> The products and services we provide will use the least amount of energy and material resources possible.

[sustainablewebmanifesto.com](https://www.sustainablewebmanifesto.com/)

---

### 03. Open

> The products and services we provide will be accessible, allow for the open exchange of information, and allow users to control their data.

[sustainablewebmanifesto.com](https://www.sustainablewebmanifesto.com/)

---

### 04. Honest

> The products and services we provide will not mislead or exploit users in their design or content.

[sustainablewebmanifesto.com](https://www.sustainablewebmanifesto.com/)

---

### 05. Regenerative

> The products and services we provide will support an economy that nourishes people and planet.

[sustainablewebmanifesto.com](https://www.sustainablewebmanifesto.com/)

---

### 06. Resilient

> The products and services we provide will function in the times and places where people need them most.

[sustainablewebmanifesto.com](https://www.sustainablewebmanifesto.com/)

---
